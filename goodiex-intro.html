Why this fits

Clothing sales ops usually leak time in the same few places

The fastest paid milestone is not a giant automation map. It is one reliable path from inquiry or order signal to a structured sales record, clean owner assignment, approved follow-up, and visible status.

01

Manual routing

Requests land in different channels, then someone has to sort product, size, delivery, customer, and owner details by hand.

02

Duplicate records

The same customer or order can appear more than once, which creates follow-up confusion and unreliable reporting.

03

Slow follow-up

Approved email or WhatsApp follow-up can be queued automatically while anything risky or unclear is held for review.

Proposed v1 workflow

One production-ready path, not a fragile demo

I would start with dummy/test records and wire this around the tools GoodieX already uses. The exact integrations depend on your current stack, but the operating shape stays the same.

Capture Form, email, Shopify/WooCommerce, sheet, or manual sales intake.
Normalize Product, customer, order, urgency, channel, and missing-field checks.
Dedupe Match existing customer/order records before creating new ones.
Route Assign owner, update the source of truth, and alert the right channel.
Follow up Queue approved messages, log status, and surface exceptions.
