# 10-Day Workflow Revenue Recovery Sprint

## Positioning

One expensive operational bottleneck fixed in 10 business days: lead response, quote intake, CRM cleanup, reporting, or AI-assisted inbox/support triage.

The buyer should already feel the pain as missed revenue, delayed quotes, messy handoffs, slow reporting, or founder time wasted on repetitive admin.

## Pricing

| Package | Price | Purpose |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Workflow Triage | USD 750 | Paid diagnosis, ROI estimate, workflow map, and implementation spec. Credited toward a sprint if started within 14 days. |
| 10-Day Sprint | USD 4,800-7,500 | One workflow implemented, tested, documented, and handed off. Includes one revision pass. |
| Optimization Retainer | USD 750-1,500/mo | Monitoring, small fixes, reporting, and workflow improvements after launch. |

To reach USD 10,000 net profit quickly, target either:

- 2 sprint clients at USD 6,000+ each, or
- 3 sprint clients at USD 4,800 each, or
- 1 higher-scope sprint plus 2 triage projects.

## Scope

Included:

- Discovery from existing form, inbox, CRM, sheet, or workflow screenshots.
- One defined workflow with clear input, processing, output, and owner.
- Working prototype or implementation.
- Error handling and rollback notes.
- Handoff guide with setup, operating instructions, and maintenance notes.
- One revision pass after first test.

Excluded unless separately scoped:

- Cold SMS, robocalls, unsolicited WhatsApp campaigns, or review-gating.
- Legal, medical, financial, or HR decision automation without human approval.
- Custom mobile apps, complex enterprise SSO, long-running data migration, or regulated data handling.
- Ongoing hosting, paid APIs, phone numbers, domains, message fees, or third-party subscriptions.

## Best Prospects

- Agencies with client workflow overflow.
- Industrial suppliers and B2B distributors with manual quote requests.
- Home services and clinics with missed calls or slow follow-up.
- Professional services teams creating repetitive proposals or reports.
- Startups hiring for ops/data/automation roles but needing backlog cleared now.

## Close Path

1. Show a tiny, relevant demo.
2. Ask for the current workflow input and destination.
3. Offer paid triage if scope is unclear.
4. Offer fixed sprint if pain and owner are clear.
5. Collect payment or signed acceptance before build.
6. Record all evidence in `data/ledger.csv`.
