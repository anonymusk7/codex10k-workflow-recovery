## Public-source scope summary

Public summaries describe a statewide integrated public defense management system covering case management, document intelligence, financial management, workload/timekeeping, attorney and client portals, court/agency integrations, workflow automation, analytics, AI/ML, and implementation/transition services.

Migration and scale facts from the public summary:

- About 840 internal users.
- About 250 contract attorneys.
- 37 offices.
- About 58,000 cases per year.
- About 154 TB FY2025 data.
- HCL Notes/Domino migration: 254 databases, 338 forms, 13,211 fields, and about 44.6M records.
