# Research Notes

Date: 2026-05-08

## Demand and Pricing Signals

- AI automation implementation pricing is commonly positioned from low-thousands per workflow to five-figure implementations.
- Public competitor pricing found by research agents included entry setup packages around USD 2,000-7,500 and larger implementations from USD 10,000+.
- Upwork reported in April 2026 that demand for top AI skills more than doubled year over year as AI becomes embedded in work.
- Current marketplace constraints favor buyer-intent channels over generic cold outreach: Upwork, n8n/Make/Zapier/Airtable communities, public hiring posts, HN, YC/Wellfound, and agency overflow.

## Recommendation

Start with a fixed-scope workflow sprint rather than broad AI consulting.

Fastest paths:

1. Public hiring posts for n8n/Zapier/Make/OpenAI contractors.
2. Agency overflow offers.
3. Warm contacts and replies.
4. Upwork proposals where the buyer has clear scope and budget.
5. Community answers that lead to invited DMs or paid triage.
