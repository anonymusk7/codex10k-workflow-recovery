Suggested workflow

Make accessibility and migration risks visible before launch

This support does not replace a full web design and CMS implementation firm. It gives the Town or selected prime vendor a focused QA layer around the highest-risk parts of the RFP: public-service findability, accessible templates, searchable documents, staff publishing, analytics readiness, and launch evidence.

01 Scope samples Agree representative page types, public documents, forms, calendar/event views, and mobile paths.
02 Audit triage Run automated checks, then manually verify the issues that matter for users and staff.
03 Migration checks Spot-check redirects, page titles, metadata, document labels, search results, and broken links.
04 Retest notes Separate found issues, fixed issues, open risk, and items that need a prime vendor decision.
05 Staff handoff Deliver a concise checklist for editors maintaining pages, links, images, forms, and PDFs.
